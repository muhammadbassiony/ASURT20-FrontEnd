Our Competitions

01. Formula Student UK

Formula Student is a student engineering competition held annually in the UK. Student teams from around the world design, build, test, and race a small-scale formula style racing car.

02. Shell Eco Marathon

Shell Eco-marathon is a world-wide energy efficiency competition sponsored by Shell. Participants build automotive vehicles to achieve the highest possible fuel.

03. ROV

“ROV” stands for remotely operated vehicle; ROVs are unoccupied, highly maneuverable underwater robots that can be used to explore ocean depths while being operated by someone at the water surface. Remotely operated vehicles, or ROVs, allow us to explore the ocean without actually being in the ocean.
