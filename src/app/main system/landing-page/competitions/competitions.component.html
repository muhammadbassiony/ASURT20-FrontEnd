Our Competitions

Formula Student UK

The competition run by the Mechanical Engineers Institution is the world’s largest student motorsport event and challenges student engineers to design, build and race a single seat racing car in one year. It takes place at Silverstone circuit, We have started participatation since 2010 in ICE(Internal Combusion Engine) catageory, and in 2019 participated in EV(Electrical Vehicle) cataegeory with sussex university.

Shell Eco Marathon

Shell Eco-Marathon challenges student teams from around the world to design, build and test ultra-energy-efficient vehicles. The winners are the teams that go the furthest using the least amount of energy. The competition is split into two classes or categories. The Prototype class focuses on maximum efficiency, while passenger comfort takes a back seat. The Urban Electric Concept class encourages more practical designs, We have started in participation in prototype cataegory in 2013, in 2017 we started in participation in urban cataegory.

ROV

The mate ROV competition is, at its core, designed to be educational and inspirational event for students. It challenges them to think of themselves as entrepreneurs and transform their teams into companies that manufacture, market, and sell “products”. ROVs can be described as underwater robots. Their performance underwater depends on hydrodynamic behavior and naval architectural principles, We have started in participation since 2014. The competition has three stages, a local one in Cairo which qualifies competitors to the regional competition, ROV Arab in Alexandria, and then the third stage in NASA USA.

EVER

With the help of the Academy of Scientific Research & Technology, Previous members of ASU racing team organized EVER. The Electric Vehicle Rally(EVER) is an energy efficiency competition. EVER is giving young engineers the opportunity to challenge and asses their technical and creative skills in designing, building and developing an urban concept of an electric vehicle,We have started in participation since 2019.




Racing Aeolus

It’s one of the 4 biggest sustainable racing events, It’s held in Den Helder Netherlands,  The idea is to develop and build Wind Powered Vehicles (WPV) that can generate energy from the wind as they drive against the wind and compete with other teams from across the globe, Entrants to the race must design and build their own single-seated, wind-powered vehicle incorporating a wind turbine that transmits the power of the wind to the wheels by electrical or mechanical means.




FSUK Artificial Intelligence

FS-AI challenges university teams to build and develop the driving systems to run a fully level 4 autonomous vehicle. It is a Real-World Autonomous event where teams must demonstrate their understanding and practical solutions for the integration of autonomous vehicles as a future transport solution, We have started in it 2020 as first time in our history we were able to get second place overall securing a top 3 finish in all events of the competition.




ASURT Seminars

In order to increase our impact on the students even more, Many seminars will be held in the faculty throughout the year to:

  • Invest in our greatest resource the students who’ll be able to continue to spread our mission.
  • Educate students about basics of automotives to discover the passion in them.
  • Let them know more about the team and what we provide from experience.
