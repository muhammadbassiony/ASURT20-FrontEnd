ROV

The Mate ROV competition is designed to be educational and inspiration event for students, It challenge them to think of themselves as entrepreneurs and transform their teams into companies that manufacture, market, and sell "products".

ROVs can be described as underwater robots, Their performance underwater depends on hydrodynamic behaviour and naval architecture principles.

The competition has three stages, a local one in Cairo which qualifies competitors to the regional competition, ROV Arab in Alexandria, and then the third stage in NASA USA. Students are challenged to complete various tasks underwater with their ROV being controlled by one of them.
